Eukcensus dataset notice

The IMG profile contains transformed records from the supporting data for
"Global Environmental Genomics Reveals Vast Uncultivated Protist Diversity and
Taxonomic Blind Spots," Zenodo record 20653200. The dataset is licensed under
Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

Dataset: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20653200
License: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
Related preprint: https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.03.26.645542

Creators: Miguel Fernando Romero Gutierrez, Arianna I. Krinos, Xyrus
Maurer-Alcala, John A. Burns, Ramunas Stepanauskas, Tanja Woyke, and Frederik
Schulz.

The IMG profile retains only `IMG_` sequence records from the composite 16S and
18S FASTA files. It replaces embedded reference records with the same SILVA
138.2 and PR2 5.1.1 sources used by the curated profile. IMG taxonomy is derived
against those updated references. Distributed IMG metadata contains only
`taxon_oid`, `latitude`, and `longitude`; project, contact, comment, and other
source fields are excluded.
